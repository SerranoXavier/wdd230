Our History

More than 25 years ago, in 1996, John Doe, the co-founder of Bountiful Foods, realized that a happy and balanced life starts with good nutrition.
Despite extensive research, he had to resolve not to be able to find a supplier who would allow him to buy fresh, local, tastefully and nutritionally excellent food for his gourmet restaurant.
Based on this observation, with his childhood friend Jane Doe, who would later become his wife, they founded Bountiful Foods on April 1, 1998.
Since then, Boutiful Foods has continued to grow and diversify. This, always with the initial aim of providing the most generous food and respecting our environment.
