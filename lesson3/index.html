BE LYON

The place to be

Chamber of Commerce - Lyon (France)

Purpose

The purpose of Be Lyon, is to promote the beautiful city of Lyon, France.

p>Lyon is rich by its economy: it is the second place in France for the surface of offices. Lyon has first class infrastructures in chemistry, biochemistry and pharmaceutical industry and many center of excellence.

Lyon is rich by its history: mankind has lived here for more than 2000 years. Lyon was built under the Roman Empire and was the capital of the Gauls.

Lyon is rich by its gastronomy: Lyon has thousands of restaurants and is well known worldwide for the "bouchons", typical family restaurants, but also for its first class chefs.

Lyon is rich by its accessibility: Lyon has two airports, 6 train stations (including the first european train station by the number of connecting travelers) and is connected to the busiest French high speed train line.

Lyon is rich by its architecture: There is 241 historic monuments in Lyon, the old district (427 ha) is classified by the UNESCO as a World Heritage Site.

Lyon is rich by its research and education: more than 155,000 students live in Lyon and attend four universities and numerous schools of higer education.

Lyon is rich by its cultural life: Lyon has more than 15 museums, numerous festivals and events all along the year, the most important being probably the "Fête des Lumières" (Lights festival) in December. The cinematograph was invented in Lyon, in 1895 by the Lumière brothers.

Lyon is rich by its people: come to visit Lyon, you will want to stay with us!

Because Lyon is such a great city, we want to help people to invest, visit and live in Lyon.
