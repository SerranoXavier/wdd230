Target Audience

The target audience is potential investors and businesses in any industry, but more specificly in chemistry, biochemistry, pharmaceutical and new technology industries. It could also be agencies and communities that want to promote the city and the surrounding area: industry, economy, tourism... Tourists and travelers are also welcome to find noticable information for their stay in the region as they could be potential customer for the referenced buisnesses.

Persona 1

Name

Léa

Age

26

Occupation

Web Designer

Location

Lille, France

Background

Léa has just finished her studies and because she can easily work remotely, she wants to travel to discover her country: France. She has never been to Lyon and she is wondering what there is to do and visit in Lyon. She also needs a place to stay for one or two weeks. Léa is interested in art, night life and gastronomy. She has no driving licence.

Scenario

  • Find information about the monuments and museums
  • Discover local specialities restaurants and night clubs
  • Find information about sightseeing spots in Lyon and nearby
  • Find reviews of hotels and bead & breakfast rents
  • Find information about the festivals held in Lyon during that period of the year

Difficulties

  • She needs a place to rest but also work nearby the center
  • She might have limited expense possibilities
  • She has no knowledge about Lyon, and needs to be well guided
  • She needs to be able to travel only by public transportation

Persona 2

Name

Andrea

Age

54

Occupation

CEO for a company in the new mobility industry

Location

Modena, Italy

Background

Andrea wants to implent his business in France and start to grow his company abroad in Europe. He is looking for a dynamic area were he could rent a small office and start to hire few employee. He is also planning on his subsidiary to grow quickly and needs a evolving environement. But it is risky and he does not know well the French culture. Lyon being the second place in France in business, and being much closer to Italy than Paris (and probably less expesive), he would like to invest in Lyon rather another city in France.

Scenario

  • Find information about the local economy, specificly in his domain
  • Receive juridical help to start a business in France
  • Find assistance to understand how to hire in Lyon
  • Find a nice office with attractive location and services
  • Discover French culture
>

Difficulties

  • He does not speak French
  • He has no clue on how to run a business in France
  • He is not sure that he can find the good talents for his business and want to operate it from taly with local human ressources
  • He does not know the local industry
